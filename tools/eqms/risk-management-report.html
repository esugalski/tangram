5 · Residual risk evaluation
ISO 14971:2019 §§7.4–8 — post-control risk levels and acceptability determination for each risk
After implementation of risk controls, residual risk was re-estimated for each risk scenario. Where residual risk falls in the tolerable zone, the risk has been evaluated against clinical benefit per ISO 14971 §8.2 (benefit-risk analysis). All risks must be within the acceptability criteria defined in RM-001 before this report can be approved.

6 · Benefit-risk analysis
ISO 14971:2019 §8 — required for residual risks in the tolerable zone and for the overall determination
Where residual risks remain in the tolerable zone after risk reduction, or where risks cannot be reduced further, a formal benefit-risk analysis is required under ISO 14971:2019 §8.2. Clinical benefits must be documented and weighed against the residual risks. If benefits do not outweigh residual risks, the device may not be placed on the market.
7 · Overall residual risk evaluation
ISO 14971:2019 §9 — the key determination required in the Risk Management Report
Per ISO 14971:2019 §9, the overall residual risk — considering all residual risks collectively — must be evaluated and determined to be acceptable before the device proceeds to production or market. This is a separate determination from the individual risk evaluations performed in §6.
