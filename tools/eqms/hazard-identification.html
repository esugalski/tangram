Hazard Identification
Purpose

This checklist implements ISO/TR 24971 Annex A — a structured series of questions to identify all device characteristics that could affect safety. It is an informative (non-mandatory) guide that complements the normative requirements of ISO 14971:2019.

How to use

Yes — This characteristic applies. Describe the specific hazard(s) in the Notes column.

Maybe — Potentially applicable or requires further investigation. Capture what you know so far.

No — Not applicable to this device. The row will dim and the Notes field will lock.

Downstream use

All "Yes" and "Maybe" responses — along with their notes — are stored in a structured format that flows into the Risk Analysis table. Export to CSV at any time for records or team review.

Completeness

ISO/TR 24971 notes this list is neither exhaustive nor representative of all devices. Add device-specific questions as needed and skip questions that are clearly not relevant. Document your rationale in the Notes column.

Reference

ISO/TR 24971:2020 Annex A (informative) — "Identification of hazards and characteristics related to safety" · ISO 14971:2019 §5.4
