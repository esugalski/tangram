GUIDANCE Development Plan
Your design & development plan is a written record that describes the design work, responsibilities, interfaces, and review points. This is typically the first formal quality record a medtech startup produces — and one of the first things auditors ask for.
Without a plan, you can't show that design activities were controlled. A missing or outdated dev plan is a common FDA 483 observation. Keeping this updated also anchors every downstream gate review.
What to include
✓Project name, PM, and plan version
✓Phases with deliverables and owners
✓Entry and exit criteria per phase
✓Named individuals in the responsibility matrix
✓Review cadence and gate criteria
✓Route for approval via e-signature
