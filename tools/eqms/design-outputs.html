What is a "Design Output"?
If design inputs are what the device must do, design outputs are how it does it — the actual drawings, specs, firmware source, schematics, labels, and IFU (instructions for use). The physical and documentary artifacts that make up your device.
Rules of thumb: (1) every output must trace to at least one design input, (2) outputs must be reviewed and approved before use in production, and (3) changes to outputs go through Change Control. Together with the inputs + V&V reports, these outputs make up the Device Master Record (DMR).
