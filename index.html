How Tangram Helps

The redo loop is what really slows down medtech.

Most redo loops start when regulatory, quality, and manufacturing decisions are made without enough regard for adoption. Tangram helps lean teams make those decisions with the commercial endgame in view from the start.

Regulatory Clarity

Choose the pathway, evidence strategy, and claims direction that support not just clearance, but a product the market can adopt.

Right-Sized Quality

Build a quality system that supports execution now, stands up later, and keeps the team focused on the work that actually moves the business forward.

Manufacturing Readiness

Make supplier, development, and production decisions that support scale, economics, and real-world adoption before they become late-stage constraints.

Founder

Eric Sugalski

Engineer, Founder, and MedTech Investor

"MedTech success does not come from technical innovation alone. It comes from aligning technology, regulatory strategy, and commercial realities from day one."

Eric is the Founder of Tangram, a medtech consultancy that helps device companies navigate FDA clearance, launch manufacturing, and build quality systems around the commercial factors that matter most. With more than 25 years of hands-on experience, he has led programs spanning surgical instrumentation, drug delivery systems, wearables, and life-support technologies.

Before founding Tangram, Eric built and led a medical device development firm for 15 years before its acquisition in 2024. He also invests in medtech ventures through Robin Hood Ventures, the largest angel investor consortium in the Mid-Atlantic region, and is committed to helping innovative teams turn device concepts into reliable, scalable, high-impact medical products.
